The Idea

Inquiry Hub was built on one idea. Students should be able to have choice and freedom to follow their interests. Here we execute this idea by having students do something called Independent Directed Studies (IDS).

An IDS is a opportunity to take a course of study the student is interested in with the supervision and support of a teacher. You can check out some of the cool things the students are doing with their IDS.

The Type of Students

Inquiry Hub is for students who share a passion for investigation and understanding, and using technology to seek answers to their questions. The students need to be able to manage themselves, work independently and be interested in research and learning with technology

Application Process

Application Process

The application process is surprisingly simple.

1.You fill out the relatively short application form.
2.You wait for Mr.Truss to contact you, and schedule an interview with him.
3.The interview with the student and parent determines if Inquiry Hub is the best place for you.

If it is, you are in!
